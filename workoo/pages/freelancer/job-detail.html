Job Summary

We are a professional group of carpenters dedicated to delivering exceptional craftsmanship and tailored solutions in the construction industry. With a combined experience of 12 years, our team specializes in a diverse range of carpentry work, including but not limited to framing, cabinetry, finishing, and renovations.

Job Duties:

The job duties of a carpenter can vary depending on the specific role and type of construction work they're involved in. Here are some common job duties:

  • Read and interpret blueprints, sketches, or building plans to understand project requirements and dimensions
  • Accurately measure, cut, and shape wood, plastic, and other materials using hand tools and power tools according to project specifications
  • Assemble and install structures and fixtures such as walls, floors, doors, windows, cabinets, staircases, roofing, and framing, ensuring they meet safety and building code standards
  • Revise the work of previous designers to create a unified aesthetic for our brand materials
  • Repair or replace damaged or old structures, fixtures, or furniture, using various carpentry techniques to restore them to their original condition
  • Perform finishing work by sanding, staining, varnishing, or painting wood surfaces to enhance their appearance and protect them from environmental elements
  • Construct frameworks, including walls, partitions, and other structures, using appropriate materials and techniques
  • Design, build, and install cabinets, shelves, and other storage units according to client specifications, ensuring functionality and aesthetic appeal
